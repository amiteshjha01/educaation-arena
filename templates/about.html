Our Story

Educaation Arena was founded by Amitesh Jha, a passionate educator and B.Tech student in Computer Science Engineering at CCSIT, TMU. What started as a vision to make quality education accessible to all has grown into a comprehensive learning platform that empowers students worldwide.

Through his experience as both a student and educator, Amitesh identified the need for practical, hands-on learning in technology education. This led to the creation of Educaation Arena, where theory meets practice in an engaging learning environment.
